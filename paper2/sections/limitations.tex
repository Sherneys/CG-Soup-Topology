\textbf{Synthetic, closed, single-machine.} All quantitative claims are on
synthetic renders of closed surfaces with known topology, on one GPU: an
analytic family plus four external genus-known meshes
(\S\ref{sec:generality}; one a raw artist mesh ingested and certified ---
the intended path for scans), still short of a public benchmark. A real
(open-surface, clinical) demonstration remains future work: scan
boundaries contaminate H1 and no clean ground-truth diagram exists; of
the concrete paths (boundary-aware persistence, filtering boundary-born
bars, template-aligned initialization) we judge bar-filtering the
pragmatic near-term step, with a synthetic open-surface stress test
first.

\textbf{Density floor.} The loss cannot see features below the significance
threshold at its sampling density (torus void; two of four double-torus
loops at $M{=}2048$); the bundle audit makes this visible, not invisible
--- but it is a real ceiling on what the objective can enforce.

\textbf{Fixed target diagram.} The target bundle presumes known ground-truth
topology (true where a prior scan or template exists; not in blind
capture). Template-free designs are natural but untested: minimizing total
persistence (risks collapsing legitimate structure), a target-\emph{genus}
penalty on the significant-H1 count (what we would try first), or
statistical priors over a shape class's diagrams.

\textbf{Control strength.} The repulsion control is aggressive (it destroys
topology at the calibrated magnitude); a gentler variant at half the radius
is still $1.6\times$ worse than baseline (sphere: $.0972$ vs $.0623$), so the
verdicts do not hinge on control tuning --- but control \emph{design}
deserves more variety than one family at two strengths.

\textbf{Nondeterminism.} DiffSoup's CUDA training is not bit-reproducible:
divergence enters at resampling, where borderline prune/split decisions
flip under floating-point drift. Tail coefficients of variation run
$7$--$10\%$ for baseline and loss arms alike, and a loss-identical re-run
pair (the sphere C6 arm, which is C1 by construction there) landed $6\%$
apart --- the scale of pure run-to-run noise. All statements are
seed-averaged with Welch statistics.

\textbf{Recruitment guarantees.} Recruitment's self-correction premise ---
unproductive edits saturate at the local detour scale --- is validated
empirically (toys, training, stress test), not proven; pathological
geometries (thin filaments, tightly constrained manifolds) could defeat
it; a failure-case study and a hardness analysis are future work.
